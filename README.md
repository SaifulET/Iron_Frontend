# 📅 Bookly — Next-Gen Service Marketplace & Booking Platform

Bookly is a modern, sleek, and highly responsive service marketplace and booking platform designed to connect customers with local service businesses. It streamlines the entire appointment lifecycle—from discovering nearby services through interactive maps to booking appointments, managing staff schedules, and overseeing business operations through powerful role-based dashboards.

### 🚀 Key Features

* **🗺️ Interactive Discovery & Maps:** Discover nearby beauty, wellness, healthcare, sports, events, and home service providers through interactive map-based search powered by geolocation technologies such as Geoapify and Mapbox.

* **📅 Frictionless Booking Experience:** A seamless booking flow featuring category sliders, custom time and clock pickers, dynamic availability checks, and real-time booking updates.

* **💼 Business Management Portal:** A dedicated workspace for business owners to manage business profiles, service catalogs, pricing, add-ons, staff, calendars, bookings, payouts, customer reviews, analytics, and secure account recovery with OTP verification.

* **👥 Staff Dashboard:** A dedicated interface that allows staff members to manage assigned appointments, view schedules and calendars, and update their personal settings.

* **🧑‍💼 Supervisor Dashboard:** Designed for team leads and supervisors to monitor staff members, review schedules, manage operational activities, and coordinate day-to-day workflows across their teams.

* **🛡️ Super Admin Dashboard:** Provides high-level platform management with centralized control over supervisors, categories, booking operations, staff management, and platform-wide performance metrics.

* **📊 Analytics & Performance:** Comprehensive insights into bookings, business performance, customer reviews, and operational metrics to help businesses make informed decisions.

* **🎨 Premium UI/UX:** Built with Next.js, React, and Tailwind CSS, featuring modern typography, clean layouts, glassmorphism-inspired design elements, smooth animations, and a fully responsive experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

### 👥 Tailored Role-Based Dashboards

Bookly provides dedicated experiences for every user type, ensuring efficient workflows and clear access control across the platform:

* **Business Owners** — Manage businesses, services, staff, bookings, schedules, payouts, reviews, and analytics.
* **Staff Members** — Manage assigned appointments, schedules, calendars, and personal settings.
* **Supervisors** — Monitor staff, coordinate schedules, and oversee team operations.
* **Super Admins** — Manage the overall platform, supervisors, categories, booking workflows, and global metrics.

By combining intelligent service discovery, streamlined booking workflows, powerful business management tools, and role-based administration, **Bookly delivers a complete digital ecosystem for customers, service providers, staff, and platform administrators.**
